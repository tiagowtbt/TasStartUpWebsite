OYE

THE FUTURE IS HERE

MODERN

The OYE sensor is the most modern in terms of mobility for people with visual impairments.

EASY TO USE

The OYE is attached to the cane and works with ultrasonic sensors that act looking for aerial obstacles such as trees, poles, parked vehicles, signs and other objects that are above the waist and that the traditional cane does not identify.

ADVANTAGES?

We have a very close contact with our customers and our price will make more people have access to our technology. The customer will only pay for the time the cane is used (in addition to receiving various benefits according to the chosen plan).
