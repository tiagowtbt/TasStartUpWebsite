ABOUT US

The Accessible Startup is a technology startup from Belo Horizonte that operates on the H2H (Human to Human) model by developing technologies that aim to break physical and social barriers preventing people from unleashing 100% of their potential.
Our mission is to create software, hardware and initiatives that contribute to making the world a inclusive home.

The idea of ​​creating a cane with sensors came up at a school science fair, and in October 2019 during the pre-acceleration program of Sebrae (AGITA), our founders created The Accessible Startup (TAS), a startup that in its creation has already emerged with the desire to impact millions of lives around the world.
